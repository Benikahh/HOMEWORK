What is Growth Mindset?

Growth Mindset is an idea developed by Standford University by Carol Dweck.This therefore,refers to the belief that the ability to learn is not fixed,that it can be changed with your effort.

What is Fixed Mindset?

Fixed Mindset is the belief that intelligence,talent and some other qualities are unchangeable.For instance,if you're not good at something,you typically think that you will never be good at it.
